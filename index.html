Research Question


Is There any correlation between people perspective regarding politics, democracy, leisure time, happines, work and other aspects related to efficiency with a prosperity of a country.


Why I ask this question? the reason is come from my curiosity to see a lot of differences between country in the world and I believe one factor that make this happen is people perspective or mindset in each country

I have read interesting book called why nations fail. This book gives several conclusions about the reason a nation can fail like ideology, geography, culture and people ignorance. Based on that, I develop a research question to see correlation between people perspective and prosperity of a country. To answer that I try to choose several countries that have gap in GDP




To see people perspective, I choose several variable related to work, happiness, politic



I checked one of variable for example important in life: Leisure time. From this variable we can see what kind of country that think leisure time is important.



Based on this value, we can see countries in Asia do not think leisure time is really important for life. To make sure this conclusion, I tried to see from map perspective



After filter the value between 0 to 50, we can see countries in Asia and North Africa have opinion that leisure time is not important in life. I try to see other variables





Based on this result, we can see all counties have different perspective towards several aspects. However, it is still hard to find the correlation between people perspective and prosperity of country. Because lack of data like GDP or life expectancy


D3.js Analytic Trail


There are several interesting facts, I have received when I did analytic trail using my d3.js Visualization. Because using parallel coordinate I can see directly all variable connection with each other that I can not see in WVS online tool.



I found country with low life expectancy, does not mean they have lower value of happiness, those countries have same index of happiness with other country.



People from country with low GDP also have lower perspective about the importance of work compare to other country.



From this data I Found people with high satisfaction about life usually come from high GDP country, but they also have perspective that leisure time is not too important for their life.




Also, when I try to see the development of a country for 20 years, I see an Interesting phenomenon, in this case Colombia, When Colombia Increase their GDP, I see improvement in other aspects of perspective. It can be 2 possibilities; first people perspective changing and make GDP increase or GDP Increase and make better lifestyle and change people perspective.



Also using this visualization, I can compare two different countries.
